Jagriti App is a web app which aims at spreading awareness among the economically weaker sections of the society about what the government policies are for them and how they can develop with the help of these policies and programs.

The app works not only as a solution to the problem of lack of awareness but also solves two other major problems. First of providing the basic necessities to the needy and second of acting as a platform where people can donate things like clothes, grocery, books etc.

How we work

  • Step 1: Scheduling Pickup

    The whole process starts with the scheduling pickup. The donor makes the donation ready and schedules a pickup for the same. Donations can be easily scheduled from the Jagriti App and the donors can choose the time slot as per their convenience.

  • Step 2: Picking Up the Donation

    As the time for the pickup approaches, a volunteer is appointed for the donation pickup. The volunteers can be either of our organization or of any of our partnered NGOs.

  • Step 3: Earning Points

    This is the stage which fulfills our main objective of spreading awareness. The donee visits the Jagrukta Zone wherein, several articles and quizzes on Government schemes are present. The donee reads the articles and later attempts a quiz. The points are awarded on the basis of the right answers.

  • Step 4: Shopping at Jagriti Store

    Here, the donee can redeem its points to buy groceries at Jagriti Store. The points' system also ensures that the dignity of the donee is not compromised.

  • Step 5: Delivering the order

    The order which is placed by the donee will then be processed and get delivered as soon as possible.
