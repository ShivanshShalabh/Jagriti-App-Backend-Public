Volunteer With Us
Who do we look for?
  • Students pursuing graduation (any stream).
  • Graduates and final year students pursuing LLB degree (Bachelor of Laws).
  • Content Writers
  • Others
Responsibilities
Undergraduates:
  • Reaching out to the people in rural areas to spread awareness about the schemes and programs for them through offline workshops on weekends.
  • Creating contents like videos, banners etc. for the awareness drives.
Law Students and Graduates:
  • Reaching out to the people in rural areas to spread awareness about the schemes and programs for them through offline workshops on weekends.
  • Doing technical stuff like researching, blogging etc.
Content Writers:
  • Writing blogs and articles on social issues in order to spread awareness.
  • Reviewing blogs and articles.
Others:
  • Assisting in organization of the awareness drives.
  • Assisting in online work.
  • Delivering and picking up donations.
  • Any other required sector in which they have expertise.

Please feel free to write to us at volunteer@jagritiapp.com for clarification of any further queries.
